Welcome To Rudra IT Hub

50 Years of experience in Education

Rudra IT hub is an IT company which is also providing IT training to IT / non-IT freshers and professionals .Our vision behind training is to enrich the knowledge & skill set of young and fresher software engineers by providing them value added training in areas of software development. This skill set will fulfill the gap area in b/w skills an IT company needs and the education that Engineering, and other degrees(BCA/MCA/Bsc. IT) colleges provide.

We believe in providing professional and quality knowledge to all students associated with us. Our Mentors with several years of industry experience in their specific field give extensive practical session to have a better understanding of the technology.
